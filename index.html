About Península Surf School

At Península Surf School, you won't just take a surf lesson: you'll learn to surf with one of the best surfers in the Dominican Republic.

We share our passion for the ocean, combining technique, safety, wave reading, and real progression. Our goal is for everyone to have an authentic, fun, and safe experience while discovering surfing on the Caribbean beaches of the Samaná Peninsula.

A true surf experience in Las Terrenas.

Lessons and Experiences

Beginner Lesson

A 2-hour session, perfect if it's your first time surfing or if you want to improve your basic skills. Ideal for families, friends, and couples who want to have fun, learn something new, and enjoy the ocean during their vacation.

Prices:

  • Private lesson: US$75
  • Lesson for 2 to 3 people: US$60 per person
  • Group lesson: US$50 per person

Lesson includes:

  • 30 minutes of instruction on the beach
  • Ocean safety and basic wave reading
  • Technique for standing up on the board
  • Practice in the water catching waves

Private Surf Coaching

Individual coaching designed for surfers who want to progress faster and take their level to the next level.

Through personalized instruction, we will work on your technique, wave selection, ocean reading, and confidence in the water. This experience is ideal for intermediate and advanced surfers looking to improve with the guidance of a local professional surfer.

Includes:

  • Personalized instruction
  • Video analysis
  • Technique and posture improvement
  • Wave selection
  • Ocean reading
  • Specific recommendations for your level

Our Team

Cristian Padilla

Cristian Padilla, born in 1997 and raised in Las Terrenas, is one of the most prominent surfers in the Dominican Republic. He started surfing at age 11 and, thanks to his discipline, perseverance, and passion for the ocean, has represented his country in international competitions in more than 15 countries, including WSL Qualifying Series events, the ISA World Surfing Games, and the ALAS Global Tour.

In 2026, he founded Península Surf School in Las Terrenas, with the purpose of sharing everything he has learned throughout a lifetime dedicated to surfing. Through his school, Cristian seeks to transmit not only technique and experience, but also respect for the ocean, confidence in the water, and a passion for the sport, helping new generations and visitors discover the world of surfing in a safe, authentic, and fun way.

Mizael Hernández

Mizael Hernández, born in 2002 and raised in Las Terrenas, is passionate about surfing and a dedicated, cheerful, and hardworking person. Since 2020, he has worked as a surf instructor, sharing his knowledge with patience, enthusiasm, and always with a smile.

His charisma and love for the sea make him an essential part of Península Surf School, where he guides students of all levels to gain confidence in the water and enjoy the process of learning to surf.

Miguel de León

Miguel de León, born in 2006 and raised in Las Terrenas, is a competitive surfer and instructor with a natural talent for sports, especially surfing. He was a member of the national team and represented the Dominican Republic at the ISA World Surfing Games Junior. He has also participated in the ALAS Global Tour, gaining experience in international competitions.

Miguel has shared training and competitions with Cristian Padilla in various locations, including Las Terrenas and Peru. Always willing to help and motivate others, he is now part of Península Surf School, where he combines his experience as a competitive surfer with his passion for teaching.
