About Peninsula

Here you won't just take a surf lesson, you'll learn to surf with one of the best surfers in the country. At Peninsula, we share our passion for surfing and will teach you technique, reading the ocean, safety, and real progression.

A true experience on the Caribbean beaches of the Samaná Peninsula.

Meet The Team

Cristian Padilla

Born in 1997 and raised in Las Terrenas, Cristian Padilla is one of the Dominican Republic's most prominent surfers. He began surfing at age 11 and, thanks to his discipline, perseverance, and passion for the ocean, has represented the country in international competitions in more than 15 countries, including WSL Qualifying Series events, the ISA World Surfing Games, and the ALAS Global Tour.

In 2026, he founded Península Surf School in Las Terrenas with the goal of sharing everything he has learned throughout a lifetime dedicated to surfing. Through his school, Cristian seeks to impart not only technique and experience, but also respect for the ocean, confidence in the water, and a passion for the sport, helping new generations and visitors discover the world of surfing in a safe, authentic, and fun way.

Miguel de León

Born in 2006 and raised in Las Terrenas, Miguel has a gift for sports, especially surfing. He was part of the national team and represented the Dominican Republic in the ISA World Surfing Games Junior, and has participated in the ALAS Global Tour.
