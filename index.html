About Peninsula Surf School

Peninsula Surf School is based at beautiful Playa Bonita in Las Terrenas on the Samaná Peninsula.

Surfing here isn’t just a lesson — it’s one of the most fun experiences you can have while visiting the area. Whether you’re travelling solo, with friends, or as a family, catching your first wave together is something you’ll never forget.

Our sessions focus on clear coaching, safety, and helping you progress quickly while keeping the relaxed Caribbean vibe that makes Las Terrenas so special.

With warm water, beautiful beaches, and beginner-friendly waves, Playa Bonita is the perfect place to learn to surf.

FAQ

Do I need experience?

Not at all. Most students are learning to surf for the first time.

How long is a lesson?

Lessons usually last around 1.5 to 2 hours. Beginner lessons include around 45 minutes of instruction on land before heading into the water.

Where are the lessons held?

Most lessons take place at Playa Bonita in Las Terrenas. On request, sessions can also be arranged anywhere on the Samaná Peninsula depending on conditions.

Do you provide surfboards?

Yes, surfboards are provided for lessons.

What should I bring?

Bring swimwear, a towel, sunscreen, and water.

How do I book?

Simply message us on WhatsApp and we’ll organise the best time for your surf session.
