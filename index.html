FAQ

Quick answers, easy planning.

Click a question to expand.

Do I need experience to take a lesson? ⌄

Nope. Beginner lessons are designed for first-timers. We start on the sand, then head into gentle water with coaching the whole time.

What’s included? ⌄

Typically: board + leash, safety briefing, technique instruction, and in-water coaching. Bring water, sunscreen, and a towel.

How long are sessions? ⌄

Most lessons are ~90 minutes. Guided sessions are usually ~2 hours depending on conditions and the group.

What should I bring? ⌄

Swimsuit, towel, reef-safe sunscreen, water, and good vibes. If you have a rash guard, bring it.

Can you choose the best time for waves? ⌄

Yes. We’ll recommend a time based on wind and swell, and pick a spot that matches your level.
