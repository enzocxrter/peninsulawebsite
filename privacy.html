Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 2026

Peninsula Surf School ("we", "our", or "us") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting any personal information you provide to us.

Information We Collect

We may collect information when you:

  • Contact us through WhatsApp, Instagram, email, or other communication channels.
  • Book a surf lesson or surf experience with us.
  • Visit our website.

The information we collect may include:

  • Your name
  • Phone number
  • Email address (if provided)
  • Booking details and preferences
  • Any information you choose to share with us

How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Respond to enquiries
  • Arrange and manage bookings
  • Communicate lesson details and schedules
  • Improve our services
  • Comply with legal obligations when required

Sharing Information

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information.

We may share information with trusted service providers only when necessary to operate our business, such as website hosting or communication services.

Website Analytics and Cookies

Our website may use cookies and similar technologies to improve your browsing experience and understand website traffic.

You can choose to disable cookies through your browser settings.

Data Security

We take reasonable steps to protect your information from unauthorized access, loss, misuse, or disclosure.

Third-Party Links

Our website may contain links to third-party websites such as WhatsApp, Instagram, or YouTube. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of those websites.

Your Rights

You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal information by contacting us.

Contact Us

If you have any questions regarding this Privacy Policy, please contact us through surf@peninsulasurfschool.com or via WhatsApp.
